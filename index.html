About Me

I am a Birmingham, United Kingdom based artist. I have been a comic book fan since the late 1970s when I started reading British publications such as TV Century, Commando and Action. Then I discovered American comic books Spider-Man and Ghostrider. I've been hooked ever since. Now I am looking to break into the comic book industry doing flats regularly and am just beginning my journey. I love European comics, American comics, Manga and general art. I am happy working on any style of art and any format, whether posters/single images, or full comic book pages.
